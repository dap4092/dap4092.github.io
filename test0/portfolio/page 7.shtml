About Me

I am a lifelong resident of Rochester, New York.

I have long been interested in video games; games having been a distinct part of my life since childhood.

I continue to enjoy games, whether its a series I've loved for years, a masterpiece I missed in the past, or a recent release.

As I got older, I gradually became more interested in how exactly how such things were created.

In these modern times, media has evolved into various interactive forms that provide many more options than the older restrictive forms of media.
